MOA Scorer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

MOA Scorer ("the app") is designed to work entirely on your device. Your privacy is simple: the app does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.

Information the app does not collect

The app has no accounts and no sign-in. It does not collect your name, email, contacts, or location, and it does not use analytics or advertising trackers.

Photos and measurements

To measure a group, the app accesses a photo you capture or select. That photo and the resulting measurements are processed on your device and stored only in the app's local storage on your device (for your saved range log). They are not uploaded to any server and are not shared with us or any third party. Deleting a saved entry, or deleting the app, removes that data from your device.

Camera and photo access

The app requests camera and/or photo access solely so you can supply a target image to measure. Images are used only for that purpose and remain on your device.

Children

The app is a general-audience utility and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? A dedicated support email address is being set up and will be posted on the support page.
